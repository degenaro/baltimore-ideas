
# 🎆 Baltimore July 4th Activities

**Baltimore’s Independence Day celebrations feature the grand return of the Inner Harbor fireworks, special 250th American milestone exhibits, daytime concerts, and scenic harbor cruises.** Events span from morning historical gatherings to late-night waterfront spectacles.

---

## 💥 Evening Fireworks & Waterfront Celebrations

The epicenter of the city's celebration takes place along the [Waterfront Promenade](https://baltimore.org) and surrounding parks:
* **Inner Harbor Kickoff (6:00 PM):** Head to the Inner Harbor Amphitheater to catch the start of the city's official festivities.
* **Live Music (7:00 PM):** Enjoy a live performance from the band *Avenue 66* along the promenade.
* **Official Fireworks Show (9:00 PM):** Watch the spectacular sky display light up over the Inner Harbor basin. 
* **Middle Branch Drone Show (9:30 PM):** Immediately following the fireworks, catch a synchronized drone light show hosted at [Middle Branch Park](https://baltimore.org).

---

## ☀️ Daytime Festivals & Cultural Events

Make the most of the holiday afternoon with these community and historical gatherings:
* **250th All-American Bash (10:30 AM):** Celebrate America's semiquincentennial at the [B&O Railroad Museum](https://bmorefamilies.com). This family-friendly event features interactive railroad exhibits, patriotic activities, and entertainment.
* **Summer in the Squares Concert (12:00 PM):** Bring a blanket or lawn chair to [Mount Vernon Place](https://facebook.com) for a free outdoor concert featuring local artists *N-CHAT*, *Noah Pierre Trio*, and *Ray Winder*.
* **Overlea Farmers Market (8:30 AM):** Pick up seasonal morning treats and socialize with neighbors at this neighborhood market on [West Overlea Avenue](https://facebook.com).

---

## 🎵 Late Night & Nightlife Activities

Keep the celebration going after the fireworks conclude:
* **BEY vs BRAT Party (9:00 PM):** Dance the night away at the [Ottobar](https://facebook.com), an 18+ event featuring a mashup of Beyoncé classics and Charli XCX tracks with cash prizes for best dressed.
* **Genesis House Music (7:00 PM):** Check out live house music mixed by Kasper Burnstein and guests at the [Next Phaze Cafe](https://eventbrite.com). 
* **Tony Woods Live (7:00 PM):** Grab tickets for an adults-only (21+) stand-up comedy performance at the [Baltimore Comedy Factory](https://baltimorecomedy.com).
